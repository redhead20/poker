Pick the best 5 You can use 0, 1, or 2 hole cards
Example: A-K-Q-J-10 would be a straight (or royal flush if same suit).

Goal

Make the best 5-card poker hand using any combo of your 2 hole cards + the 5 community cards.

You: 2 cards Board: 5 cards

Rounds

  1. Preflop – everyone gets 2 cards, betting starts
  2. Flop – 3 community cards, betting
  3. Turn – 4th community card, betting
  4. River – 5th community card, betting
  5. Showdown – best hand wins

Table Actions

Check (0 chips)

Bet (first money in)

Call (match bet)

Raise (increase bet)

Fold (quit hand)

All-in (all chips)

You can only check if nobody has bet yet that round.

Blinds

Small Blind and Big Blind are forced bets before cards are played. They create action so the pot grows.

Preflop action starts left of the Big Blind.
